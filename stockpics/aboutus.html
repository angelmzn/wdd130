Our History

Everything began with our founder, Angel Manzano, when he was trying to find images for a youtube video. He was having a hard time to find stock images that capture what he was trying to express, so he decided to go take his own photos. Eventually, he'd have a wide range of images for every situation, and that's what we are sharing with you.
